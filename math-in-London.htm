Come to London and enrich the standard content of mathematics classes by gaining global perspective!


  • Earn six Miami University credits in Mathematics while living in London, United Kingdom, for 5 weeks.
  • Satisfy the Global Perspectives Miami Requirement, which takes 9 credit hours if taken at Miami University.
  • Students will have long weekends for exploring London and  independent travel in Europe.
  • Classes are taught by Miami  faculty, cover the same material as when taught at Miami University, and are enriched by cultural international experience.
  • Classes are held at Richmond University, Kensington.
  • The program is organized by American Institute for Foreign Study.

Included in the program are:
  • Housing in student residence within central London, with fully equipped kitchens, linens and free WiFi;
  • London travel pass;
  • half-day bus tour of London;
  • welcome and farewell dinners;
  • ticket for a West End production;
  • trip to Oxford University;
  • trip to Brighton;
  • ticket for a flight on the London Eye;
  • and more...

Click here for a slide presentation about the program.

The two mathematical courses which will be offered in London  are MTH 435 Mathematical Modeling Seminar MPC and your choice of   MTH 411 Foundations of Geometry or MTH410 Topics in Geometry. The classes satisfy Miami  Global Perspectives requirement and can count toward

  •       a thematic sequence     
  •     requirements for a minor in Mathematics
  •        or requirements towards a major in Mathematics or Mathematics and Statistics

 

Prerequisites will depend on the subjects and may include Linear Algebra, MTH 222  and Calculus III (MTH 252) or permission of the instructor.

 

Contact us if you are interested in going but do not have Calculus III, we will let you know what you can do to get the permission to participate.


The classes are scheduled to provider 4 day long weekends for personal travel.
