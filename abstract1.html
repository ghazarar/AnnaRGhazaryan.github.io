We study a mathematical model for high Lewis number combustion processes with the reaction rate  of the form of an Arrhenius law with or without an ignition cut-off.  An efficient method for the proof of  the existence and uniqueness of combustion fronts is provided by geometric singular perturbation theory.   The fronts supported by the model with very large Lewis numbers are small perturbations of the front  supported by the model with inﬁnite Lewis number.
